Merchant Self-Onboarding

Onboarding​

Displays onboarding instructions before starting merchant registration.

  • Shows prerequisites for registration.
  • Includes CNIC availability instruction.
  • Advises proper lighting and location for capturing images.
  • Merchant proceeds by selecting OK.

Attach CNIC​

Captures CNIC images for verification.

  • Front side CNIC image capture.
  • Back side CNIC image capture.
  • Merchant proceeds by selecting CONTINUE.

  • Once the images are scanned, CNIC details are auto fetched via OCR call.

CNIC Details​

Displays extracted CNIC information.

  • Name.
  • Father/Husband Name.
  • CNIC Number.
  • Date of Birth.
  • Gender.
  • CNIC Issuance Date.
  • CNIC Expiry Date.
  • Displays scanned CNIC images.
  • Merchant proceeds by selecting CONTINUE.

Business Details​

Captures merchant business information.

  • Display Name.
  • Doing Business As (Store/Shop Name).
  • Contact Number.
  • NTN (Optional).
  • Nature of Business.
  • Merchant proceeds by selecting CONTINUE.

Business Address​

Captures business address details.

  • Region selection.
  • City entry.
  • Business Address entry.
  • Merchant proceeds by selecting CONTINUE.

Attach Files​

Captures supporting verification images.

  • Signature image upload.
  • Live image capture.
  • Shop image upload.
  • Merchant proceeds by selecting CONTINUE.

Biometric Authentication​

Performs biometric authentication.

  • Fingerprint authentication required.
  • Successful authentication confirmation displayed.

Add Bank Account​

Captures settlement bank account details.

  • Bank selection.
  • Account Number / IBAN entry.
  • Account Title entry.
  • Cheque image upload.
  • Merchant proceeds by selecting CONTINUE.

Onboarding Status​

Displays onboarding completion status.

  • Shows onboarding progress.
  • Displays completion status.
  • Merchant proceeds by selecting DONE.

Dashboard​

Displays merchant home screen after onboarding.

  • Greeting with merchant name.
  • Daily sales amount displayed.
  • Feature shortcuts available:
    • Raast QR
    • Raast RTP
    • Transaction History
