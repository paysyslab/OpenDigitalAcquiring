RAASTPOSHybrid (RAAST + POS)

Request To Pay Later On-us Transaction

Functional Flow​

Requester Action(s)Process Description
Merchant• Selects RTP-Later generate option.
• Initiates the RTP-Later request towards Acquiring Services.
Acquiring Services• Receives the RTP-Later request.
• Initiates Title Fetch (IBAN/Alias) request to RAAST OC.
RAAST OC• Receives Title Fetch request.
• Sends the TF-CAS request to RAAST SBP.
RAAST SBP• Orchestration: Acknowledges receipt to RAAST OC.
• Forwards Title Fetch Request to the Acquiring Bank/Issuer CBS.
Acquiring Bank CBS• Processes Title Fetch and returns Success Response (Account Title) to RAAST SBP.
RAAST SBP• Forwards the Success Response back to RAAST OC.
RAAST OC• Validates response and returns the Unique RTP ID and Title to Acquiring Services.
Acquiring Services• Generates RTP-Later Payload with the unique ID.
• Initiates the official RTP-Later request to RAAST OC.
RAAST OC• Receives request and sends pain.13 (Request for Payment) to RAAST SBP.
RAAST SBP• Validation: Checks RTP ID uniqueness and stores the record.
• Returns HTTP 200 Acknowledgement to RAAST OC.
RAAST OC• Receives storage confirmation and shares the acknowledgement with Acquiring Services.
Issuer• Retrieval: Fetches RTP-Later request (get-mx) from RAAST SBP.
• Customer Action: Customer reviews and Approves the request.
• Initiation: Sends pacs.008 (RTP-Later Payment) to RAAST SBP.
RAAST SBP• Validates pacs.008 against the original pain.13.
• Fetches pacs.002 (ACSP) and acknowledges receipt to the Issuer.
Issuer• Receives final acknowledgements and processes the Debit Authorization.
RAAST OC• Fetches pacs.14 from RAAST SBP.
• Identifies non-Acquiring Bank BIC (Off-Us).
• Initiates "Notify Merchant" call to Acquiring Services and validates RTP ID.
Acquiring Services• Verifies Merchant Status and RTP ID.
• Notifies the Merchant and sends success back to RAAST OC.
Merchant• Receives real-time In-App notification of the payment initiation.
RAAST OC• Fetches CB.pacs.008 from RAAST SBP.
• Clearing: Initiates Fund Transfer to Acquiring Bank CBS (GL to GL).
  o Entry: Debit RAAST GL / Credit Merchant Payable GL.
Acquiring Bank CBS• Processes GL transfer and returns response to RAAST OC.
RAAST OC• Logs transaction and initiates MerchantandPaymentNotificationOffUs to Acquiring Services.
Acquiring Services• Settlement Calculation: Calculates Net Amount (Deducting FED + MDR).
• Initiates CreditAdviceOffUs request to RAAST OC.
RAAST OC• Sends final Fund Transfer Request (strfundtransfer) to Acquiring Bank CBS.
Acquiring Bank CBS• Final Settlement:
  o Debit: Merchant Payable GL Account.
  o Credit: Merchant Account.
RAAST OC• Confirms success of creditAdviceOffUs to Acquiring Services.
Acquiring Services• Sends final Payment Successful notification to the Merchant.
Merchant• Receives final confirmation: "Payment Processed Successfully."
