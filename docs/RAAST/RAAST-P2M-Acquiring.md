# Introduction to RAAST-P2M

## Introduction:

The State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) to improve the payment infrastructure of the country by developing digital financial services, reducing reliance on cash, and driving financial inclusion in Pakistan, recently introduced RAAST. RAAST will become a core component of Pakistan’s payment infrastructure that will enable banks and other regulated entities to offer its individual and business customers to make any payment digitally in a simple, fast, low-cost, and secure manner. Following will be the core benefits of RAAST to the bank:

## Instant Payments: 

Real-time digital payments for across individuals, merchants, businesses, and government entities. Following types of payments will be supported by the platform in context of a PSP:

### Merchant or Bill Payments
- Customer initiated Push payments.
  - Static QRC
  - Payment by Alias
- Merchant initiated Request to Pay (RTP)
  - RTP Now by IBAN or RAAST ID
  - RTP Later by IBAN or RAAST ID
  - Dynamic QRC
- Payments initiated from a PISP.
- Real-time Clearing of PAYPAK Card transactions
 

## Interoperability: 

The platform will allow all financial institutions to seamlessly connect to each other via a single link to the central infrastructure, making digital payments accessible across any channel to customers of any financial institution

## Customer Centric: 
The platform is built on cutting-edge technological standards allowing banks to develop innovative and user-friendly digital payments products and services. (e.g. payments through phone number and email)

## Reliable & Secure: 
The platform will provide more security where the payer will authorize each transaction.
