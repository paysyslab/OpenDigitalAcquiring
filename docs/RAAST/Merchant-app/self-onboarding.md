# Merchant Self-Onboarding 

## Onboarding

Displays onboarding instructions before starting merchant registration.

- Shows prerequisites for registration.
- Includes CNIC availability instruction.
- Advises proper lighting and location for capturing images.
- Merchant proceeds by selecting **OK**.

  ![OpenConnect – RAAST Integration Architecture](/img/Onboarding/2.png)
</div>

## Attach CNIC

Captures CNIC images for verification.

- Front side CNIC image capture.
- Back side CNIC image capture.
- Merchant proceeds by selecting **CONTINUE**.

<div style={{ width : '30%' }}>
  ![OpenConnect – RAAST Integration Architecture](/img/Onboarding/3.png)
</div> 
<div style={{ width : '30%' }}>
  ![OpenConnect – RAAST Integration Architecture](/img/Onboarding/7.png)
</div>

- Once the images are scanned, CNIC details are auto fetched via OCR call.

## CNIC Details

Displays extracted CNIC information.

- Name.
- Father/Husband Name.
- CNIC Number.
- Date of Birth.
- Gender.
- CNIC Issuance Date.
- CNIC Expiry Date.
- Displays scanned CNIC images.
- Merchant proceeds by selecting **CONTINUE**.

## Business Details
