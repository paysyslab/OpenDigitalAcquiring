# Merchant App Dashboard

The **Dashboard** is the primary landing screen of the Merchant App, providing merchants with a quick overview of their financial activity and direct access to key payment features. It is designed to give immediate visibility into earnings while enabling fast navigation to transaction-related functionalities.

### Key Features

- **Sales Summary**
  - Displays today’s total sales amount prominently.
  - Shows associated settlement account/IBAN for reference.
  - Quick refresh option available for updated balance view.

- **Quick Access Features**
  - Raast QR: Access QR-based payment acceptance functionality.
  - Raast RTP: Initiate Request-to-Pay transactions.
  - Transaction History: Navigate directly to past transaction records.

- **Notification & Menu Access**
  - Notification icon for alerts and important updates.
  - Menu access for additional app navigation and settings.

- **Promotional / Informational Banner**
  - Displays relevant business messages, tips, or promotional content.
  - Helps merchants stay informed about platform updates or offers.

This dashboard acts as the central operational hub, enabling merchants to monitor earnings and access payment services efficiently from a single screen.
